Samuel Roberts is a Great-Great-Great-Great-Grandfather of Faye, Jenny and Kate Lloyd and of Chloe and Daniel Marshall.

He married Mary Jarrett and they had at least 1 child, William.

The only reference to him is the Birth Certificate of his son William who was born on 30th January 1849. He was living at 1 Wharf Street, Brinnington, Stockport, Cheshire and worked as a Weaver.
