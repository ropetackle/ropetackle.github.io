FRANCES MARTHA CLIO ROBERTS

Introduction and Links To Her Other Pages

Frances Martha Clio Roberts is a Great-Great-Grandmother of Faye, Jenny and Kate Lloyd and of Chloe and Daniel Marshall.

 

Frances Martha Clio Roberts in about 1890

Introduction

Frances Martha Clio Roberts was the only child of William Roberts and his wife, Fanny and was born in Woolwich, London in either 1870 or 1871.

She married David Tate and they had 8 children. These were:-

  • Florence
  • John
  • Robert Frederick
  • Edward Reginald
  • Dorothy Alma
  • Marjorie
  • Victor Stuart
  • George James Graham

She died at Hastings on 13th May 1947 aged 76.
