It was while Frances Martha Clio Roberts was working as a Housemaid for a Mrs. Barram of Stratford House, Stainbeck Lane, Chapeltown, Leeds, that she met David Tate. He was working as a Domestic Gardener at a house in Weetwood Lane, Headingley, Leeds.

Frances became pregnant and she and David Tate married at Leeds Register Office on 17th November 1894.
When Frances left Mrs. Barram employment she was given a Bible, which Frances's granddaughter Helen Kingdon has. It is inscribed:-

"To David and Fanny Tate with best wishes from Mrs. Henry Barram. Jan 1st 1895".

Her first child, Florence, was born soon after her marriage, on 31st January 1895. Frances and David were living at 5 Highbury Lane, Headingley, Leeds and he was still working as a Domestic Gardener.

Frances had 8 children. These were:-

  • Florence
  • John
  • Robert Frederick
  • Edward Reginald
  • Dorothy Alma
  • Marjorie
  • Victor Stuart
  • George James Graham
