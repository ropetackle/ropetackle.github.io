Mary Jarrett is a Great-Great-Great-Great-Grandmother of Faye, Jenny and Kate Lloyd and of Chloe and Daniel Marshall.

She married Samuel Roberts and they had at least 1 child, William.

The only reference to her is the Birth Certificate of her son William who was born on 30th January 1849. She was living at 1 Wharf Street, Brinnington, Stockport, Cheshire and her husband was a Weaver.
