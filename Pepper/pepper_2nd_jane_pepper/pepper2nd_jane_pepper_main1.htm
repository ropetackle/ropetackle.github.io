Born: 1850 in Swepstone, Leicestershire
 
Parents: Thomas Pepper and Alice Sharp
 
Brothers and Sisters: Fanny,  William,  Robert,  Elizabeth,  Esther,  Thomas

Jane Pepper was born in Swepstone, Leicestershire 1850. She was the seventh child of Thomas Pepper, an Agricultural Labourer, and Alice Sharp.

Jane had at least three brothers and three sisters. These were:-

Fanny, William, Robert, Elizabeth, Esther and Thomas

The only reference to Jane is in the 1851 Census. She was then 8 months old and living with her parents in Swepstone village.
