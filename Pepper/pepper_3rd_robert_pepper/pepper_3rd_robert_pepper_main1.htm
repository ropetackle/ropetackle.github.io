Born: About 1881 or 1882 in Nottingham
 
Parents: Robert Pepper and Ann Hodges
 
Brothers and Sisters: Alice,  Eliza,  Florence

Robert John Henry Pepper was born in about 1881 or 1882 in Nottingham. He was the third child of Robert Pepper, a Railway Labourer, and Ann Hodges.

Robert had three sisters. These were:-

Alice Mary, Eliza and Florence

The first reference to Robert is the 1891 Census. He was 9 years old and living with his mother at 25 Newbridge Street, Meadows, Nottingham. His mother worked as a Hosiery Mender.
His father had died in December 1882, just after Robert was born.

By the time of the 1901 Census it would seem that Robert's mother had died as Robert, whose age is given as 17 years old, was living at 12 Newthorpe Street, Nottingham, the home of his married sister Alice. Robert was single and working as a Pawnbroker's Assistant.
There are two errors in his entry in the Census Return. He is described as being 17 years old which can't be correct as this would mean that he was born in 1883 or 1884, well after his father's death in 1882. Also his first name is given as "Jno", which is short for "John", and not as "Robert".

Peggy Davies, the daughter-in-law of Robert's sister Florence, thinks that he died when he was quite young, possibly aged 20 or 21.
