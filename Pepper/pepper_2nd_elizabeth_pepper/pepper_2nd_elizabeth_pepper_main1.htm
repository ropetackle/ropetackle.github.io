Born: About 1841 in Swepstone, Leicestershire
 
Parents: Thomas Pepper and Alice Sharp
 
Brothers and Sisters: Fanny,  William,  Robert,  Esther,  Thomas,  Jane

Elizabeth Pepper, also known as Bessy, was born in Swepstone, Leicestershire in about January 1841. She was the fourth child of Thomas Pepper, an Agricultural Labourer, and Alice Sharp.

Elizabeth had at least three brothers and three sisters. These were:-

Fanny, William, Robert, Esther, Thomas and Jane

The first reference to Elizabeth is in the 1841 Census. She was 5 months old and living with her parents at  Dishley, in the Parish of Swepstone.
Dishley Farm is about 1km. from Swepstone and it is assumed that her father was working at that farm at the time.

When her sister Esther was born in February 1843 Elizabeth and her family were living in Swepstone.

The next reference to Elizabeth is in the 1851 Census. She was now 10 years old and living with her parents in Swepstone village.
