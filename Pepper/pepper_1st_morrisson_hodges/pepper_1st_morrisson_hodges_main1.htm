Relationship: Morrisson Hodges is a Great-Great-Great-Great-Grandfather of Chloe and Daniel Marshall.
 
Wife: Ann Crump
 
Children: Ann

Morrisson Hodges married Ann Crump and they had at least 1 child, Ann.

The only reference to Morrisson is in the Birth Certificate of his daughter Ann who was born on 22nd June 1846. when she was born he was a Needle Maker and living in Basford, Nottingham.

Morrisson is next mentioned in October 1870 when his daughter Ann married Robert Pepper. The Marriage Certificate shows that he was still a Needle Maker.
