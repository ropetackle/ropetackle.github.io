Born: 1843 in Swepstone, Leicestershire
 
Parents: Thomas Pepper and Alice Sharp
 
Brothers and Sisters: Fanny,  William,  Robert,  Elizabeth,  Thomas,  Jane

Esther Pepper was born in Swepstone, Leicestershire 21st February 1843. She was the fifth child of Thomas Pepper, an Agricultural Labourer, and Alice Sharp.

Esther had at least three brothers and three sisters. These were:-

Fanny, William, Robert, Elizabeth, Thomas and Jane

The first reference to Esther is her Birth Certificate. When she was born her parents were living in Swepstone and her father was working as an Agricultural Labourer.

The next reference to Esther is in the 1851 Census. She was now 8 years old and living with her parents in Swepstone village.
