Born: About 1847 or 1848 in Swepstone, Leicestershire
 
Parents: Thomas Pepper and Alice Sharp
 
Brothers and Sisters: Fanny,  William,  Robert,  Elizabeth,  Esther,  Jane

Thomas Pepper was born in Swepstone, Leicestershire in about 1847 or 1848. He was the sixth child of Thomas Pepper, an Agricultural Labourer, and Alice Sharp.

Thomas had at least two brothers and four sisters. These were:-

Fanny, William, Robert, Elizabeth, Esther and Jane

The only reference to Thomas is in the 1851 Census. He was then 3 years old and living with his parents in Swepstone village.
