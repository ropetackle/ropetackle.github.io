Relationship: Ann Crump is a Great-Great-Great-Great-Grandmother of Chloe and Daniel Marshall.

Ann Crump was the wife of Morrisson Hodges and they had at least 1 child, Ann.

The only reference to Ann Crump is on the Birth Certificate of her daughter Ann who was born on 22nd June 1846. She was living in Basford, Nottingham and her husband was a Needle Maker.
