Born: About 1851 or 1852 in Bottesford, Leicestershire
 
Parents: William Marshall and Mary Lamb
 
Brothers and Sisters: Thomas,  John,  George,  Ellen

William Marshall was born at Easthorpe, Bottesford, Leicestershire, in either 1851 or 1852. He was the second child of William Marshall, a Bricklayer, and Mary Lamb. He was Christened at Bottesford Church on the 8th February 1852.

He had at least three brothers and one sister. These were:-

Thomas, John, George and Ellen

The only reference to him is in the 1861 Census. He was then 8 years old and living with his parents, brothers and sister, and maternal grandmother, Ann Lamb, at the Beershop, Easthorpe, Bottesford. It is possible that this was the licensed premises in Church Street, where his maternal Grandfather, Thomas Lamb, had been the Publican. There is currently a Public house, The Red Lion, on the corner of Church Street and Grantham Road which might have been where he was living.
