Alan Marshall was the second child of Thomas Robert Marshall (known as Tommy) and Margaret Ethel Davies (known as Peggy)

He was born in St Helens on the 29th December 1941 while his parents were living at 4 The Grove, Bleak Hill, St Helens

He had two brothers. These were:-

Robin and Brian

When World War II ended the family moved to 203 Dentons Green Lane, St Helens where Alan lived until he left home to go to University.

After leaving Cowley Grammar School, St Helens, Alan went to the University College Of North Staffordshire, now Keele University, where he studied Chemistry and Economics.

After graduating he went to work at Tube Investments as a management trainee and then to Cadbury's at Bournville. It was while he was at Cadbury's that he met Brita Sterkel. They married in Germany on 15th April 1971.

In the meantime Alan had joined the Burton Group in Leeds and when they married they set up home in the Leeds Area.
