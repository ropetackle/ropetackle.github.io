When they married, Alan and Brita bought a house at 16 Linton Road, Shadwell, Leeds.

Alan's daughter, Kirsten, was born on 7th September 1979.

Alan eventually left Burton's and became an Independent Financial Adviser.

He died on 26th June 2003 after a long and painful battle against Cancer.
