Relationship: Thomas Marshall is a Great-Great-Great-Great-Great-Grandfather of Chloe and Daniel Marshall.
 
Children: William

Nothing is known about where or when Thomas was born although it was possibly in Nottinghamshire or Leicestershire. In the 1861 Census manuscript entry the the birth place of his son is given as Nottinghamshire at a place that could be either Eaton, Eston or Exton. It is hard to decipher the writing. Neither is it known who his parents were nor who his wife was.

Thomas had at least one child, William.

The only reference to Thomas is in 1850 on the Marriage Certificate of his son William. He is described as being a farmer and deceased.
