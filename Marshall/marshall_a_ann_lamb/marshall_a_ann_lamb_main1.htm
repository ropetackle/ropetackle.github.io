Relationship: Great-Great-Great-Great-Great-Grandmother of Chloe and Daniel Marshall
 
Born: Bottesford, Leicestershire in about 1797 or 1798
 
Husband: Thomas Lamb
 
Children: Mary

Ann Lamb was born at Easthorpe, Bottesford, Leicestershire in about 1797 or 1798. Her maiden name is unknown and nothing is known about her parents.

She married Thomas Lamb, a Publican in Bottesford, and they had at least one child, Mary.

The earliest reference to Ann is in the 1841 Census. She was 40 years old and living with her husband and daughter in Church Street, Easthorpe, Bottesford. Her husband is described as a Publican.
There is now what was originally an old Public House, The Red Lion, on the corner of Church Street and Grantham Road and this may have been where they were living and where her husband carried out his business.

Ann is next mentioned in the 1861 Census. She was 63 years old and living with her daughter and son-in-law, William Marshall, at the Beershop, Easthorpe. She was a widow and her profession is given as that of a Nurse. She is also described as being blind. It is possible that the Beershop was where her husband had carried out his business of Publican.
