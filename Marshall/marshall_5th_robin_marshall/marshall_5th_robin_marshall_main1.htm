Robin is the eldest child of Thomas Robert Marshall (known as Tommy) and Margaret Ethel Davies (known as Peggy). He was born in Glasgow on 1st February 1939.

He had two brothers. These were:-

Alan and Brian

When Robin was born his parents were living in Hillington, Glasgow at 50 Gladsmuir Road. His father worked for Triplex Northern, a subsidiary of the glass making  company Pilkington Brothers, as their Commercial Representative in Scotland.

When it became clear later in 1939 that war with Germany was inevitable Robin's father was moved to St Helens, Lancashire where the Head Office of the Pilkington Group was located. The family settled at 4 The Grove, Bleak Hill, St Helens and they lived there throughout the War.

After the War the family moved to 203 Dentons Green Lane, St Helens and it was their that Robin lived until he started work in 1961.

Robin's first went to Rivington Road Infants School St Helens and then to Knowsley Road Junior School. At the age of 11 he took the 11 plus examination and having passed this went to Cowley Boys Grammar School where he eventually entered the 6th Form and he took "A" Levels in Maths., Physics and Chemistry.

In 1957 Robin left school and went to what was then known as the University College of North Staffordshire. This is now known as Keele University.

Originally Robin intended to take a degree in Chemistry and Physics. However in those days the degree course at Keele was a mandatory 4 year course. The first year, known as the Foundation Year, was the same for all undergraduates whether they intended to take an Arts, Science or Humanities Degree and consisted of lectures and tutorials in all the subjects covered by the courses that Keele offered. As a result of this introduction to new subjects he changed his mind about studying Physics and took his degree in Chemistry and Biology.

On Graduating in 1961 Robin joined May & Bakers, a pharmaceutical company at Dagenham, Essex, as a Works Chemist.

It was while Robin was at May and Bakers that he met Helen Marjorie Kingdon who was working there as a Personnel Assistant.

They were married at Romford Register Office on 4th July 1964.
