Brian Marshall is the third child of Thomas Robert Marshall (known as Tommy) and Margaret Ethel Davies (known as Peggy)

He was born in St Helens on the 12thAugust 1944 while his parents were living at 4 The Grove, Bleak Hill, St Helens.

He has two brothers. These are:-

Robin and Alan

When World War II ended the family moved to 203 Dentons Green Lane, St Helens where Brian lived until he left home to go to University.

After leaving Cowley Grammar School, St Helens, Brian went to Newcastle University where he studied Medicine.

After qualifying as a Doctor, Brian spent several sabbatical months at the Bird Observatory on Fair Isle and it was while he was there that his father died. Brian returned home for a few months to be with his Mother and took a temporary medical post at Ormskirk Hospital. It was while he was there that he met Linda Williams who was also working there as a Nurse. They married at Upholland Church, Lancashire on 2nd June 1973
