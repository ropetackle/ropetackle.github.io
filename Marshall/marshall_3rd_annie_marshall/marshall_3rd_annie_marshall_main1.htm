Born: About 1891 or 1892 in Wilford, Nottingham
 
Parents: Thomas Marshall and Elizabeth Russell
 
Brothers and Sisters: George,  Mary,  Thomas,  William,  Thirza,  Elizabeth,  Charlotte

Annie Marshall was born in Nottingham, possibly at 6 Skelton Terrace, Hawthorne Street, Wilford, in either 1891 or 1892. She was the eighth child of Thomas Marshall, a Bricklayer, and Elizabeth Russell.

She had three brothers and four sister. These were:-

George, Mary, Thomas, William, Thirza, Elizabeth and Charlotte

The only reference to Annie is in the 1901 Census. She was then 9 years old and living with her parents at 6 Cary Cottages, Hawthorne Street, Wilford, Nottingham.
The house appears to have been a typical C19th working class house, probably a “2 up and 2 down”.
