Relationship: Mrs. Marshall is a Great-Great-Great-Great-Great-Grandmother of Chloe and Daniel Marshall.

 
Husband: Thomas Marshall
 
Children: William

The forename and maiden name of Mrs. Marshall are not known. It is thought that she may have originated from somewhere in the Bottesford area of Leicestershire or from Nottinghamshire. In the 1861 Census manuscript entry the the birth place of her son is given as Nottinghamshire at a place that could be either Eaton, Eston or Exton. It is hard to decipher the writing.

All that is known about her is that she was the wife of Thomas Marshall, a farmer and that they had at least one child, William.
