Born: About 1889 or 1890 in Wilford, Nottingham
 
Parents: Thomas Marshall and Elizabeth Russell
 
Brothers and Sisters: George,  Mary,  Thomas,  William,  Thirza,  Elizabeth,  Annie

Charlotte Marshall was born in Nottingham, possibly at 6 Skelton Terrace, Hawthorne Street, Wilford, in either 1889 or 1890. She was the seventh child of Thomas Marshall, a Bricklayer, and Elizabeth Russell.

She had three brothers and four sister. These were:-

George, Mary, Thomas, William, Thirza, Elizabeth and Annie

The first reference to Charlotte is in the 1891 Census. She was then 1 year old and living with her parents at 6 Skelton Terrace, Hawthorne Street, Wilford, Nottingham. The house appears to have been a typical C19th working class house, probably a “2 up and 2 down”.

When the 1901 Census was taken Charlotte was 11 years old. She was still living with her parents at 6 Cary Cottages, Hawthorne Street, Wilford, Nottingham.
The address is given as 6 Cary Cottages and not 6 Skelton Terrace. It is not known if these were the same or different houses. However, in view of the fact that the numbers are the same and that the 1891 Census Returns do not mention any Cary Cottages in Hawthorne Street, it is almost certain that they were one and the same house.
