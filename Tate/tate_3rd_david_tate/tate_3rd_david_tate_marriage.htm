Pictures of Him
It was while David Tate was working as a Domestic Gardener at a house in Weetwood Lane, Headingley, Leeds, that he met Frances Martha Clio Roberts, who was a housemaid at Stratford House, Stainbeck Lane, Chapeltown, Leeds. They became intimate and Frances became pregnant. They married at Leeds Register Office on 17th November 1894.

He and Frances had eight children. These were:-

  • Florence
  • John
  • Robert Frederick
  • Edward Reginald
  • Dorothy Alma
  • Marjorie
  • Victor Stuart
  • George James Graham

When David and Frances's first child, Florence, was born in January 1895, soon after their marriage, they were living at 5 Highbury Lane, Headingley, Leeds and he was still a Domestic Gardener.

By the time that David's next child, John, was born in October 1896 they had move to Fulneck, Pudsey and he was now the Golf Professional at Fulneck Golf Club.

When their third child, Robert Frederick was born the family had again moved, this time to South Wales where he had taken up employment as the Golf Professional at Ashburnham Golf Club. They were living at 11 Carway Street, Pembrey, Carmarthenshire.

David's next two children, Edward and Dorothy, were also born at Pembrey. Marjorie, Victor and Graham were born at 38 Brynglas, Pen-y-darren, Merthyr Tydfil.
