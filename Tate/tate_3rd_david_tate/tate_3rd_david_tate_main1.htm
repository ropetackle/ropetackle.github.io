David Tate is a Great-Great-Grandfather of Faye, Jenny, and Kate Lloyd and of Chloe and Daniel Marshall.

 

David Tate in 1933

Introduction

David Tate was the seventh child of George Tate and Elizabeth Huggon and was born on 18th July 1867 at Rickerby, Cumberland.

He had six brothers and three sisters. These were:-

  • Thomas
  • Betsy
  • James
  • George
  • Mary
  • Edward
  • John
  • Sarah
  • Robert

He married Frances Martha Clio Roberts and they had eight children. These were:-

  • Florence
  • John
  • Robert Frederick
  • Edward Reginald
  • Dorothy Alma
  • Marjorie
  • Victor Stuart
  • George James Graham

He died at Pen-y-darren, Merthyr Tydfil on 23rd December 1943 aged 76.
