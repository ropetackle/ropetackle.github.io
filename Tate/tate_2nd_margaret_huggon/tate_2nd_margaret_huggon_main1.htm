Margaret Huggon was born in in Cumberland, probably in Longtown, in about 1832 or 1833. She was a daughter of James Huggon, a Cotton Weaver, and his wife Elizabeth.

She had at least one brother and one sister. These were:-

  • Edward
  • Elizabeth

The only reference to Margaret Huggon is in the 1841 Census. She was then 8 years old and living in Netherby Street, Longtown.

Her mother died in 1837 when Margaret was 1 or 2 years old
