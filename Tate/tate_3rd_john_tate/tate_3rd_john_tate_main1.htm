Description of Rickerby

John Tate was born between 1869 and 1871 at Rickerby, Cumberland. He was the eighth child of George Tate and Elizabeth Huggon.

He had six brothers and three sisters. These were:-

  • Thomas
  • Betsy
  • James
  • George
  • Mary
  • Edward
  • David
  • Sarah
  • Robert

John Tate was born at Rickerby in the Parish of Stanwix, Cumberland.
Rickerby is on the north bank of the River Eden, about 1/2 mile east of Carlisle and just off the A689. The village "belonged" to the MacInnes family who lived at Rickerby House.
Descriptions of Rickerby, based on the 1881 and 1891 Census Returns and other documents, are given elsewhere in this family history.

The earliest reference to John is in the 1871 Census. He was 1 years old, and living with his parents in Rickerby.

The next mention of John is in the 1881 Census. He was 10 years old and still living with his parents at Rickerby.

Ten years later, when the 1891 Census was taken, John was now 21 years old, working as a Commercial Clerk, and still living at home with his parents in Rickerby.
