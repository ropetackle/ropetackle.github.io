Elizabeth Huggon is a Great-Great-Great-Great-Grandmother of Faye, Jenny and Kate Lloyd and of Chloe and Daniel Marshall.

Elizabeth Huggon (maiden name not known) was born in about 1803 or 1804, almost certainly in the Longtown area. 

 She married James Huggon and they had at least three children. These were:-

  • Edward
  • Elizabeth
  • Margaret

The only reference to Elizabeth is her Death Certificate. She died from Consumption at Longtown on 7th September 1837 aged 34. Her Death Certificate gives her Occupation as that of a Weaver.
