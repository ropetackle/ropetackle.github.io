Description of Rickerby

Edward Tate was born in about 1865 or 1866 at Rickerby, Cumberland. He was the sixth child of George Tate and Elizabeth Huggon.

He had six brothers and three sisters. These were:-

  • Thomas
  • Betsy
  • James
  • George
  • Mary
  • David
  • John
  • Sarah
  • Robert

Edward Tate was born at Rickerby in the Parish of Stanwix, Cumberland.
Rickerby is on the north bank of the River Eden, about 1/2 mile east of Carlisle and just off the A689. The village "belonged" to the MacInnes family who lived at Rickerby House.
Descriptions of Rickerby, based on the 1881 and 1891 Census Returns and other documents, are given elsewhere in this family history.

The earliest reference to Edward is in the 1871 Census. He was 5 years old, and living with his parents in Rickerby.

Edward is next mentioned in the 1881 Census. He was now 15 years old, still living with his parents at Rickerby and was working as a Railway Clerk.

Edward had at least one child, a daughter Dora, whose husband was Jack Coulthard, the writer of the letter to Marjorie Tate which gave details of the parents of Edward's father, George.
