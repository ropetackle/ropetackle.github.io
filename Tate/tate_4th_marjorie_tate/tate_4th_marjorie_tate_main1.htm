Introduction and Links To Her Other Pages

Marjorie Tate is a Great-Grandmother of Faye, Jenny and Kate Lloyd and of Chloe and Daniel Marshall.

 

Marjorie Tate In About 1917

Inroduction

Marjorie Tate was the sixth child of David Tate, a Golf Professional, and Frances Martha Clio Roberts and was born on 29th January 1905 at 38 Bryn Glas, Pen-y-darren, Merthyr Tydfil, South Wales.

She had five brothers and two sisters. These were:-

  • Florence
  • John
  • Robert Frederick
  • Edward Reginald
  • Dorothy Alma
  • Victor Stuart
  • George James Graham

She married Edward John Kingdon and they had one child, Helen Marjorie.

She died on 29th March 1988 at Ilkley, West Yorkshire.
