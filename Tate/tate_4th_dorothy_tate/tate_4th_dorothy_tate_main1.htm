Dorothy Tate in About 1917

Dorothy Alma Tate was the fifth child of David Tate and Frances Martha Clio Roberts. She was born at Burry Port, Carmarthenshire in 1902. When she was born her father was the Golf Professional at Ashburnham Golf Club.

She had five brothers and two sisters. These were:-

  • Florence
  • John
  • Robert Frederick
  • Edward Reginald
  • Marjorie
  • Victor Stuart
  • George James Graham

She married George Atterbury and they had one daughter, Sheila.
