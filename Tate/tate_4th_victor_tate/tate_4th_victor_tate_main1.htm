Victor Tate In About 1917

Victor Stuart Tate was the seventh child of David Tate and Frances Martha Clio Roberts. He was born at Pen-y-darren, Merthyr Tydfil, South Wales on 6th July 1907. When he was born his father was the Golf Professional at Morlais Castle Golf Club, Merthyr Tydfil

He had four brothers and three sisters. These were:-

  • Florence
  • John
  • Robert Frederick
  • Edward Reginald
  • Dorothy Alma
  • Marjorie
  • George James Graham

He married Annie Ainsworth and they had one son, Lewis.

He died on 1st December 1953.
