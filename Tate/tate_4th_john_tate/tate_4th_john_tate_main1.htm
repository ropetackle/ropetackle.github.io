John Tate in About 1917

John Tate was the second child of David Tate and Frances Martha Clio Roberts. He was born near Leeds at Fulneck, which is near Pudsey, on 6th October 1896.

He had four brothers and three sisters. These were:-

  • Florence
  • Robert Frederick
  • Edward Reginald
  • Dorothy Alma
  • Marjorie
  • Victor Stuart
  • George James Graham

He married Elsie Davies and had one son, John

He died on 5th August 1981.

The earliest reference to John is his Birth Certificate. He was born on 6th October 1896 just to the South of Leeds at Fulneck near Pudsey. When he was born his father was the Golf Professional at Fulneck Golf Club.

In January 1898 John's father became the Golf Professional at Ashburnham Golf Club, which is at Burry Port, Camarthenshire, South Wales.

The next mention of John is in the 1901 Census. He was 5 years old and living with his parents, his sister Florence and two brothers, Robert and Edward, at 11 Carway Street, Pembrey, Carmarthenshire.
