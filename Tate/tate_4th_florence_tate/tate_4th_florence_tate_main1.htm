Florence Tate was the eldest child of David Tate and Frances Martha Clio Roberts. She was born on 31st January 1895 at 5 Highbury Lane, Headingley, Leeds.

She had five brothers and two sisters. These were:-

  • John
  • Robert Frederick
  • Edward Reginald
  • Dorothy Alma
  • Marjorie
  • Victor Stuart
  • George James Graham

She married Aubrey Charles Muscutt and they had two children:-

  • Robert Frederick
  • Muriel Joyce

The earliest reference to Florence is her Birth Certificate. She was born on 31st January 1895 at 5 Highbury Lane, Headingley, Leeds. This was two months after her parents married. When she was born her father was working as a Domestic Gardener.

At some time between her birth and October 1896 Florence's father changed his job and became the Golf Professional at Fulneck Golf Club, which is near Pudsey, Leeds and they moved to live at Fulneck.

In January 1898 Florence's father became the Golf Professional at Ashburnham Golf Club, which is at Burry Port, Camarthenshire, South Wales.

The next mention of Florence is in the 1901 Census. She was 9 years old and living with her parents and three brothers, John, Robert and Edward, at 11 Carway Street, Pembrey, Carmarthenshire.
