Description of Rickerby

James Tate was born in about 1856 or 1857 at Longtown, Cumberland. He was the third child of George Tate and Elizabeth Huggon.

He had six brothers and three sisters. These were:-

  • Thomas
  • Betsy
  • George
  • Mary
  • Edward
  • David
  • John
  • Sarah
  • Robert

James Tate was born in Longtown, which is on the A7, about 6 miles north of Carlisle, and near the Scottish Border.

Some time between about 1863 and 1866 the Tate family moved from Longtown to the small Estate village of Rickerby, in the Parish of Stanwix.
Rickerby is on the north bank of the River Eden, about 1/2 mile east of Carlisle and just off the A689. The village "belonged" to the MacInnes family who lived at Rickerby House.
Descriptions of Rickerby, based on the 1881 and 1891 Census Returns and other documents, are given elsewhere in this family history.

The only reference to James is in the 1871 Census. He was 14 years old, working as a Gardener and living with his parents in Rickerby.
