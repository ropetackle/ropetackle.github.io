Description of Rickerby

Robert Tate was born between 1876 and 1877 at Rickerby, Cumberland. He was the tenth child of George Tate and Elizabeth Huggon.

He had six brothers and three sisters. These were:-

  • Thomas
  • Betsy
  • James
  • George
  • Mary
  • Edward
  • David
  • John
  • Sarah

Robert Tate was born at Rickerby in the Parish of Stanwix, Cumberland.
Rickerby is on the north bank of the River Eden, about 1/2 mile east of Carlisle and just off the A689. The village "belonged" to the MacInnes family who lived at Rickerby House.
Descriptions of Rickerby, based on the 1881 and 1891 Census Returns and other documents, are given elsewhere in this family history.

The earliest reference to Robert is in the 1881 Census. He was 4 years old, and living with his parents in Rickerby.

Robert is next mentioned in the 1891 Census. He was 14 years old, working as a Commercial Clerk, and still living with his parents at Rickerby.

He had at least one child, a daughter Edith.
