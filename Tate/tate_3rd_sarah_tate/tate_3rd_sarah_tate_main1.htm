Description of Rickerby

Sarah Tate was born between 1872 and 1873 at Rickerby, Cumberland. She was the ninth child of George Tate and Elizabeth Huggon.

She had seven brothers and two sisters. These were:-

  • Thomas
  • Betsy
  • James
  • George
  • Mary
  • Edward
  • David
  • John
  • Robert

Sarah Tate was born at Rickerby in the Parish of Stanwix, Cumberland.
Rickerby is on the north bank of the River Eden, about 1/2 mile east of Carlisle and just off the A689. The village "belonged" to the MacInnes family who lived at Rickerby House.
Descriptions of Rickerby, based on the 1881 and 1891 Census Returns and other documents, are given elsewhere in this family history.

The only reference to Sarah is in the 1881 Census. She was 8 years old, and living with her parents in Rickerby.
