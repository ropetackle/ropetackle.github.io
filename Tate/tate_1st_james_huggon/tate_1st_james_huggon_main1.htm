James Huggon is a Great-Great-Great-Great-Grandfather of Faye, Jenny and Kate Lloyd and of Chloe and Daniel Marshall.

James Huggon born in Cumberland, probably the Longtown area, in 1805 or 1806.

James and his wife Elizabeth had at least three children. These were:-

  • Edward
  • Elizabeth
  • Margaret

The only reference to James is in the 1841 Census. He was then 35 years old, working as a Cotton Weaver and living in Netherby Street, Longtown, Cumberland.

His wife died in 1837, aged 34. When he registered Elizabeth's death he "made his mark" with an X  and gave his occupation as that of Weaver
