George James Graham Tate was the eighth child of David Tate and Frances Martha Clio Roberts. born at 38 Brynglas, Pen-y-darren, Merthyr Tydfil, in 1913. When he was born his father was the Golf Professional at Morlais Castle Golf club, Merthyr Tydfil.

He had four brothers and three sisters. These were:-

  • Florence
  • John
  • Robert Frederick
  • Edward Reginald
  • Dorothy Alma
  • Marjorie
  • Victor Stuart

He married Gwyneth Jones who died at the age of 45 in 1958. They had two children:-

  • Stewart
  • Margaret
