Edward Reginald Tate

Edward Reginald Tate was the fourth child of David Tate and Frances Martha Clio Roberts. He was born at Burry Port, Camarthenshire in 19000 or 1901.

He had four brothers and three sisters. These were:-

  • Florence
  • John
  • Robert Frederick
  • Dorothy Alma
  • Marjorie
  • Victor Stuart
  • George James Graham

He married Margaret Williams and they had five children. These were:-

  • Maurice
  • Graham
  • Barbara
  • Robert
  • Alan

He died in October 1967

The earliest reference to Edward is the 1901 Census. He was 5 months old and living with his parents and two brothres, John and Robert, and one sister, Florence, at 11 Carway Street, Pembrey, Carmarthenshire. His father was the Golf Professional at Ashburnham Golf Club.
