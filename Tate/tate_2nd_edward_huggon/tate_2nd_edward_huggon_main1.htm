Longtown

Edward Huggon was born in Cumberland, probably in Longtown, in about 1826 or 1827 He was a son of James Huggon, a Cotton Weaver, and his wife Elizabeth.

He had at least two sisters. These were:-

  • Elizabeth
  • Margaret

The only reference to Edward is in the 1841 Census. He was then 14 years old, living in Netherby Street, Longtown and like his father working as a Cotton Weaver.

His mother died in 1837 when he was 10 or 11 years old.
