My grandmother, Frances Tate, outside 38 Brynglas Street, Pen-y-Darren, Methyr Tydfil. (about 1940?) Here she is in working cap and pinny, but she tried to dress up smartly for special occasions. She was a kind, sensitive lady but rather disorganised. Her husband used to call her McGinty - I don't know why! She worked hard all her life, bringing up 8 children. She died on a visit to us in Hastings in 1947 (Helen Kingdon)
