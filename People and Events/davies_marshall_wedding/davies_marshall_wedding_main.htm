This photograph was taken after the wedding of my Mother and Father on 19th June 1937
On the back row, from left to right, are my Grandpa Davies, Grandma Marshall, Grandma Davies and Grandpa Marshall.

They are standing in the garden of Fireman's Cottage where Grandma and Grandpa Davies were living. I can remember spending many happy hours playing in the garden - in particular picking and eating the glorious raspberries that Grandpa Davies grew. (Robin Marshall)
