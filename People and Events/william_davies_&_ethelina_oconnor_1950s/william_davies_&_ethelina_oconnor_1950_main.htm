This photograph of Grandma and Grandpa Davies was taken at 1 Berrington's Lane by the wife of her brother, Alfred Hartley O'Connor, in the 1950s

On the back of the photograph it says:-
"Came across the film in one of the cameras we had with us on our trip. Vera snapped this one which I think is excellent. I was rather surprised they came out at all after all this time"
On the back the picture is dated 19th March 1957 (Robin Marshall)
