This artist's drawing shows the 1st Battalion, Royal Scots Fusiliers, taking part in the defence of Jemappes Bridge on the Mons-Conde Canal in the Battle of Mons on 23rd August 1914.
My Grandpa Marshall almost certainly would have been involved in this battle as he went to France with the Battalion at the start of the War. (Robin Marshall)
