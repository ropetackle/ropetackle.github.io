A Studio Portrait, believed to be of James Kingdon, taken in about 1900.
It is possible that it was either taken when he was 21 or when he became engaged to Elizabeth Ann Smith. He was obviously lodging with the Smiths in Helston at the time

I'm not sure who this smartly dressed young man was, possibly Bessy's father or, more probably, Dad's father (Helen Kingdon)
