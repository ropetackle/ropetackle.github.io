A studio portrait of my Mother, Peggy Davies, taken in 1934 when she was 21. The portrait is signed "Yours Sincerely Peggy 1934". On the back it says " Miss Peggy Davies (Mrs Marshall after) while she was at our office, shorthand typist with Agnes, then 1st comptometer op. with me"

(Robin Marshall)
