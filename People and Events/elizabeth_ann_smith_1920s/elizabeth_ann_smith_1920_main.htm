Dad's mother, Bessy Kingdon, in later life (about 1920?). She suffered from heart trouble and had been considered very delicate as a child. Dad said she had had smallpox and was very self-conscious of the marks, but you can't see them in this picture. I like her hair ribbon and smart blouse and skirt. Photo taken in Newton Abbot. Dad once took me to Newton Abbot and showed me the house in Powderham Terrace where they lived. We also saw her gravestone in the cemetery nearby. (Helen Kingdon)
