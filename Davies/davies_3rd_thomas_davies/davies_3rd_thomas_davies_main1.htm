Born: About 1885 or 1886 in Liverpool
 
Parents: Edward Davies and Margaret Pennill
 
Brothers and Sisters: William,  Caroline,  Marion,  Edward,  Georgina,  Winifred

Thomas Davies was the fourth child of Edward Davies and his wife, Margaret Pennill

He was born in Liverpool in about 1885 or 1886.

He had six brothers and sisters. These were:-

William, Caroline,  Marion,  Edward,  Georgina,  and Winifred

The earliest reference to him is in the 1891 Census. He was 5 years old and living with his parents, brothers William and Edward and sisters Caroline and Marion at 9 Elm Grove, West Derby, Liverpool, the house of his maternal Grandmother, Marion Pennill. 

Thomas was still living with his parents at 9 Elm Grove when the 1901 Census was taken. He was now 15 and working as an Office boy.

All that is known about him is that he married a rich widow.
