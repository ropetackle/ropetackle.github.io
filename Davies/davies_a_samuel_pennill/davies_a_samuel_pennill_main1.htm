Relationship: Samuel Pennill is a Great-Great-Great-Great-Great-Grandfather of Chloe and Daniel Marshall
 
Wife: (not known)
 
Children: William Pennill

Samuel Pennill is thought to have come from the Market Drayton area of Shropshire. Nothing is known about his parents nor his wife.

He had at least one son, William.

The only reference to Samuel is in 1852 in the Marriage Certificate of his son William. In this he is described as a "Malster". This possibly should have been Maltster.
