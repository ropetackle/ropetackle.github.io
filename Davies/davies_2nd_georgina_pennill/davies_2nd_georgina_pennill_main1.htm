Born: Between 1868 or 1870 in Liverpool
 
Parents: William Pennill and Marion McAllister
 
Brother and Sister: Margaret,   Alfred

Georgina is a daughter of William Pennill, a Printer Machinist, and his wife Marion McAllister.

She was born in Liverpool between 1868 and 1870 and had a least one older sister and brother. These were:-

Margaret and Alfred

The first reference to her is in the 1881 Census. She was then 11 years old and living with her family at 9 Elm Grove, Edge Hill, Liverpool.

She was still living at 9 Elm Grove when the 1891 Census was taken. She was now 22 years old and married to William H Stothart, a Printer. They shared the house with her widowed mother and the family of her elder sister Margaret.
