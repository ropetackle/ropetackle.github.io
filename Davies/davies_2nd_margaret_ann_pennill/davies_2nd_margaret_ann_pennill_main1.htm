Relationship: Margaret Ann Pennill is a Great-Great-Great-Grandmother of Chloe and Daniel Marshall
 
Born: Liverpool in 1853
 
Parents: William Pennill and Marion McAllister
 
Brother and Sister: Alfred,   Georgina
 
Husband: Edward Davies
 
Children: William,  Caroline,  Marion,  Thomas,  Edward,  Georgina,  Winifred

Margaret Ann Pennill who was the eldest daughter of William Pennill, a Printer, and his wife Marion McAllister, was born in Liverpool on 5th July 1853. 

She had at least one brother, Alfred, and one sister, Georgina.

Margaret married Edward Davies, a Bricklayer, who came from Anglesey and they had 7 children. These were:-

William,  Caroline,  Marion,  Thomas,  Edward,  Georgina,  and Winifred

The first reference to Margaret is her Birth Certificate. She was born at 28 Hill Street, Liverpool on 5th July 1853. When he registered her birth her father gave his occupation as that of a Printer.

The next reference to Margaret is in the 1881 Census. She was living with her parents, her husband Edward and their two children, William and Caroline at 9 Elm Grove, Edge Hill, Liverpool. Also living with them were her brother Alfred, her sister Georgina and a lodger, Margaret Stephenson, a 57 year old Tailoress from Ireland.

Margaret, Edward and her family were still living at 9 Elm Grove when the 1891 Census was taken. By now she had three more children, Marion, Thomas and Edward. Also living in the house were her widowed mother, Marion McAllister, Margaret's sister Georgina also Georgina's husband.

Margaret and her family were still at 9 Elm Grove when the 1901 Census was taken. By then her  mother had died and Margaret had two more children, Georgina and Winifred.
