Bill Davies In 1945

Born: 17th February 1910
  
Parents: William Davies and Ethelina O'Connor
 
Sister: Margaret Ethel (Peggy)
 
Wife: Lillian
 
Children: Geoffrey, Trevor and Glynn

William Alfred Davies (known as Bill) was the elder child of William Davies and Ethelina O'Connor.

He was born at Edge Street, Nut Grove, St Helens on 17th February 1910.

Soon after he was born his parents moved to a nearby new house at Palatine Villas, Rainhill Road. Subsequently they moved to Fireman's Cottage in the grounds of the Rainhill Mental hospital when his father became the Chief Officer of the Hospital Fire brigade.

He had one sister, Margaret Ethel (known as Peggy

He qualified as a Pharmacist and during World War II he served as a Sergeant Dispenser in the Royal Army Medical Corps. After the war he had a Chemist's Shop in St Helens.

He married Lillian [Maiden name not Known] and they had three children. These were:-

Geoffrey, Trevor and Glynn

For a number of years he and his family lived with his parents at 158 Speakman Road, St Helens.

He was not a very good business man and eventually he ran into financial difficulties mainly because the business could not support the life style he led. He subsequently was employed as a Pharmacist in several places in the South of England, finally settling in Milton Keynes where he was living when he died.
