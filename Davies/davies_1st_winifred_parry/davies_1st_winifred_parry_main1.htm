Relationship: Winifred Parry is a Great-Great-Great-Great-Grandmother of Chloe and Daniel Marshall
 
 Born: Between 1811 and 1812 at Newmarket (Trelawnyd), Flintshire, North Wales
 
Husband: Thomas Davies
 
Children: John,  Margaret,  Robert,  Thomas,  Winifred,  William,  Edward

Winifred Parry was born in Newmarket, (now known as Trelawnyd), Flintshire, North Wales in about 1811 or 1812. Nothing is known about her parents.

She married Thomas Davies, a bricklayer who also came from Newmarket, and they had at least 7 children. These were:-

John,  Margaret,  Robert,  Thomas,  Winifred,  William,  and Edward

Winifred's first six children were all born in Newmarket and it seems that it was some time between about 1847 and 1850 that she and her family moved to Amlwch, Anglesey.

The first reference to her is in the Birth Certificate of her son Edward who was born in 1850.
She, Thomas and their family were living in New Street, Amlwch and Thomas was employed as a Bricklayer. He does not seem to have been able to write, instead of signing the Birth Register he "made his mark" with a X.

Winifred is next mentioned in the 1851 Census. She was then 39 years old and still living with Thomas and their seven children, in Amlwch.
