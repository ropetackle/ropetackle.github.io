Peggy Davies in 1934

Relationship: Peggy Davies is a Great-Grandmother of Chloe and Daniel Marshall.
 
Born: 19th January 1913
 
Parents: William Davies and Ethelina O'Connor
 
Brother: William Alfred (Bill)
 
Husband: Thomas Robert Marshall (Tommy)
 
Children: Robin, Alan and Brian

Margaret Ethel Davies, known as Peggy, was the second child of William Davies, a Male Nurse at Rainhill Mental Hospital, and Ethelina O'Connor.

She was born at Upper Palatine Villas, Rainhill Road, Nut Grove, St Helens on 19th January 1913.

She had one brother, William Alfred (known as Bill)

The house at Upper Palatine Villas was one of a row of substantial semi-detached houses that had just been built by the Hospital and were amongst the first houses in the area to have electricity. The houses no longer exist, newer houses having been built on the site although, in November 1999, the original gate-posts of the front paths were still there.

When Peggy left school she went to work for St Helens Corporation in the the office of their Transport Department.

Peggy was a keen cyclist and a member of the St Helens section of the Cyclist Touring Club and it was through this interest that she met Thomas Robert Marshall (known as Tommy) who had joined the Club when he came to St Helens to train to Be the Commercial Representative for Triplex Northern in Scotland. they became engaged and she and Tommy married at Rainhill Parish Church on 19th June 1937

When she married she was living with her parents in a cottage known as Fireman’s Cottage in the Hospital grounds on the East side of Rainhill Road. There is now no trace of this cottage. They had moved there when her father became the Chief Officer of the Hospital's fire brigade.

Although she was living with her parents at Fireman's Cottage when she married, the address on her Marriage Certificate is given as 39 Rainhill Road. The reason for this was that she wanted to get married at Rainhill Parish Church but unfortunately her home was just outside the Rainhill Parish boundary. To get round this she left a suitcase at the house of a friend who lived in the Parish at 39 Rainhill Road and gave that as her home address.
