After Peggy married Tommy Marshall at Rainhill Parish Church on 19th June 1937 they set up home Hillington, Glasgow at 50 Gladsmuir Road. Their home was a couple of streets away from where her in-laws lived in Bearford Drive.

It was here that her first child, Robin, was born on 1st February 1939. 

Later that year, because of the imminent threat of war, her husband was called back to St Helens and Peggy and her family returned there. They went to live at 4 The Grove, Bleak Hill. It was there that her other two sons were born, Alan on 29th December 1941 and Brian on 12th August 1944.

After the War had ended Peggy and her family moved to 203 Dentons Green Lane and they lived there until she and Thomas moved to 117B Higher Lane, Rainford in the 1960s.

Following the sudden death of Tommy in July 1970 Peggy moved to Ilkley, West Yorkshire. Her eldest son Robin was living there and Alan lived nearby in Leeds.
