Born: 1842 or 1843 at Newmarket, Flintshire, North Wales
 
Parents: Thomas Davies and Winifred Parry
 
Brothers and Sisters: John,  Margaret,  Robert,  Winifred,   William and Edward

Thomas Davies was born in Newmarket (now known as Trelawnyd), Flintshire in about 1842 or 1843. His parents were Thomas Davies and Winifred Parry. His father was a Bricklayer and both his parents also came from Newmarket. It seems that some time between about 1847 and 1850 his parents moved to Amlwch, Anglesey since when his youngest brother Edward was born in 1850 they were living in New Street, Amlwch.

He had at least four brothers and two sisters. These were:-

John, Margaret, Robert, Winifred, William and Edward.

The only reference to him is in the 1851 Census. He was then 8 years old and living with his parents in Amlwch.
