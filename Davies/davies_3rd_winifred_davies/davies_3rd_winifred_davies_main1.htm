Born: About 1892 or 1893 in Liverpool
 
Parents: Edward Davies and Margaret Pennill
 
Brothers and Sisters: William,  Caroline,  Marion,  Thomas,  Edward,  Georgina

Winifred Davies was the youngest child of Edward Davies and his wife, Margaret Pennill

She was born in Liverpool in about 1895 or 1896.

She had six brothers and sisters. These were:-

William, Caroline,  Marion,  Thomas,  Edward,  and Georgina

The earliest reference to her is in the 1901 Census. She was then 5 and living with her parents at 9 Elm Grove, West Derby, Liverpool.

When she married, date unknown, the Davies family were living at 137 Alderson Road, Liverpool. She married at the Church in Alderson Road and her niece, Peggy Davies, was a bridesmaid. Her husband was called Lawrence and they had two girls and a boy.
