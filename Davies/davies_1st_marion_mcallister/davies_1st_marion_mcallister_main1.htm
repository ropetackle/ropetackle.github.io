Relationship: Marion McAllister is a Great-Great-Great-Great-Grandmother of Chloe and Daniel Marshall
 
Born: About 1831 to 1833 in Liverpool
 
Parents: Andrew McAllister (mother unknown)
 
Husband: William Pennill
 
Children: Margaret Ann,  Alfred,  Georgina,
 
Died: Between 1891 and 1901

Marion McAllister was born in Liverpool between 1831 and 1833. She was the daughter of Andrew McAllister, a Bootmaker. Nothing is known about her mother.

She married William Pennill, a Printer Machinist, who came from Market Drayton, Shropshire and they had at least 3 children. These were:-

Margaret Ann,  Alfred,  and Georgina.

The first reference to Marion is in her Marriage Certificate when she married William Pennill in Liverpool on 31st May 1852.

When Marion's daughter Margaret Ann was born on 5th July 1853, she and William were living at 28 Hill Street, Liverpool.

When the 1881 Census was taken, Marion was 48 years old and living with her husband William at 9 Elm Grove, Edge Hill, Liverpool. Also living in the house were her daughter Margaret, Margaret's husband Edward Davies and Margaret's two eldest children, William and Caroline, Marion's son Alfred, her daughter Georgina and a lodger Margaret Stephenson, a Tailoress aged 57 from Ireland.

Marion is next mentioned in the 1891 Census. She was now 59 years old and a widow. She still lived at 9 Elm Grove with her daughter Margaret, son-in-law Edward Davies and their 5 children, William, Caroline, Marion, Thomas and Edward. Also living with her was her married daughter Georgina and son-in-law William Stothart.
