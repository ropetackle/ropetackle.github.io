Born: 1837 or 1838 at Newmarket, Flintshire, North Wales
 
Parents: Thomas Davies and Winifred Parry
 
Brothers and Sisters: John,  Robert,  Thomas,  Winifred,  William and Edward

Margaret Davies was born in Newmarket (now known as Trelawnyd), Flintshire in about 1837 or 1838. Her parents were Thomas Davies and Winifred Parry. Her father was a Bricklayer and both her parents also came from Newmarket. It seems that some time between about 1847 and 1850 her parents moved to Amlwch, Anglesey since when her youngest brother Edward was born in 1850 they were living in New Street, Amlwch.

She had at least five brothers and one sister. These were:-

John, Robert, Thomas, Winifred, William and Edward.

The only reference to her is in the 1851 Census. She was then 13 years old, at school and living with her parents in Amlwch.
