Born: About 1865 or 1866 in Liverpool
 
Parents: William Pennill and Marion McAllister
 
Sisters: Margaret,   Georgina

Alfred is the son of William Pennill, a Printer Machinist, and his wife Marion McAllister.

He was born in Liverpool in about 1865 or 1866 and had a least 2 sisters. These were:-

Margaret and Georgina

The only reference to him is in the 1881 Census. He was then 15 years old and living with his parents and sisters at 9 Elm Grove, Edge Hill, Liverpool. Also living there were his sister Margaret, her husband Edward Davies, and her two eldest children, William and Caroline. There was also a lodger, a 57 year old Tailoress called Margaret Stephenson.
