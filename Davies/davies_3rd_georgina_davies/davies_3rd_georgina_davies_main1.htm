Born: About 1892 or 1893 in Liverpool
 
 Parents: Edward Davies and Margaret Pennill
 
Brothers and Sisters: William,  Caroline,  Marion,  Thomas,  Edward,  Winifred

Georgina Davies was the sixth child of Edward Davies and his wife, Margaret Pennill

She was born in Liverpool in about 1892 or 1893.

She had six brothers and sisters. These were:-

William, Caroline,  Marion,  Thomas,  Edward,  and Winifred

The earliest reference to her is in the 1901 Census. She was then 8 and living with her parents at 9 Elm Grove, West Derby, Liverpool.

Her husband was called Barnes.
