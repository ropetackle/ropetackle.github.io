Born: About 1829 or 1830 in Market Drayton, Shropshire

Wife: Marion
 
Children: Margaret Ann ,  Alfred,  Georgina

William Pennill was born in Market Drayton, Shropshire in about 1829 or 1830. His father was Samuel Pennill who is described on William's Marriage Certificate as a "Malster", possibly this should be "Maltster". Nothing is known about his mother.

William and his wife Marion McAllister had at least 3 children children. These were:-

Margaret Ann,   Alfred,  and Georgina.

The earliest reference to William is in his Marriage Certificate when he married Marion McAllister in Liverpool on 31st May 1852. His profession is given as that of a Printer.

When William's daughter Margaret was born on 5th July 1853 he and Marion were living at 28 Hill Street, Liverpool. William was still working as a Printer.

William is next mentioned in the 1881 Census. He was then 51 years old, employed as a Printer Machinist and living with his wife Marion at 9 Elm Grove, Edge Hill, Liverpool. Also living in the house was his daughter Margaret, Margaret's husband Edward Davies and Margaret's eldest two children, William and Caroline, his son Alfred, daughter Georgina and a lodger Margaret Stephenson, a Tailoress aged 57 from Ireland.

He died between 1881 and 1891.
