Born: In 1889 or 1890 in Leeds
 
Parents:  James Daniel O'Connor and Alice Louisa Williams
 
Sister and Brothers:  Ethelina,   James,   Edward,   Robert,   Alfred Hartley and Joseph Somerville

Claude O'Connor was the sixth child of James Daniel O'Connor and Alice Louisa Williams. He was born in Leeds in either 1889 or 1890.

He had one sister and five brothers. These were:-

Ethelina,   James,   Edward,   Robert,   Alfred Hartley and Joseph Somerville

The first reference to Claude is in the 1891 Census. He was 1 year old and his family were living at 4 Bywater Street. His father worked as a Mechanical Engineer.

When the 1901 Census was taken he 11 years old and still living at 4 Bywater Street. His father was now working as a Joiner.
There had, however, been a change in the family, his mother had died the previous November.
