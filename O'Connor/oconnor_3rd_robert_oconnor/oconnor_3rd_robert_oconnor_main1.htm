Robert O'Connor In 1940

Born: 1885 or 1886 in Leeds
 
Parents:  James Daniel O'Connor and Alice Louisa Williams
 
Sister and Brothers:  Ethelina,   James,   Edward,   Alfred Hartley,   Claude and Joseph Somerville

Robert O'Connor, known as Bob, was the fourth child of James Daniel O'Connor and Alice Louisa Williams. He was born in Leeds either 1885 or 1886.

He had one sister and five brothers. These were:-

Ethelina,   James,   Edward,   Alfred Hartley,   Claude and Joseph Somerville

When his brother Alfred was born in 1888, Robert and his family were living at 16 Bywater Street, Leeds and his father worked as a Mill Labourer.

The earliest reference to him is the 1891 Census. He was 5 years old and living with his family at 4 Bywater Street, Leeds. His father was now employed as a Mechanical Engineer.

When the 1901 Census was taken he 15 years old and working as a Butcher Apprentice. He was still living at 4 Bywater Street and his father was now working as a Joiner.
There had, however, been a change in the family, his mother had died the previous November.

He never married.
