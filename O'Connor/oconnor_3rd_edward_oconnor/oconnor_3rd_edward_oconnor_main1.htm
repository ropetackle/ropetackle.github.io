Born: In either 1883 or 1884 in Leeds
 
Parents:  James Daniel O'Connor and Alice Louisa Williams
 
Sister and Brothers:  Ethelina, James, Robert, Alfred Hartley, Claude and Joseph Somerville

Edward O'Connor, known as Ted, was the third child of James Daniel O'Connor and Alice Louisa Williams. He was born in Leeds in either 1883 or 1884.

He had one sister and five brothers. These were:-

Ethelina, James, Robert, Alfred Hartley, Claude and Joseph Somerville

When his younger brother Alfred Hartley was born in 1888 the family were living at 16 Bywater Street, Leeds and his father was a Mill Labourer.

The only reference to him is in the 1891 Census. He was 7 years old and his family were living at 16 Bywater Street, Leeds. By the time of the 1901 Census he had left home.
