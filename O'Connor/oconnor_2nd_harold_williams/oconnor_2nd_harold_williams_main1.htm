Parents: Richard Williams and Louisa Williams
 
Sisters: Mary and Alice Louisa

It is thought that Richard Williams and his wife Louisa had a son, Harold, as well as their two daughters, Mary and Alice Louisa. However this is not known for certain and there is no mention of him in the 1881 Census Return for Alfred Cross Street, Leeds where Louisa was living at that time.

Louisa's great-granddaughter, Peggy Davies, vaguely remembers the name Harold being mentioned in connection with Scarborough.
