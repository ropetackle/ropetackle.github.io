Born:  28th January 1888 in Leeds
 
Parents:  James Daniel O'Connor and Alice Louisa Williams
 
Sister and Brothers:  Ethelina,  James,  Edward,  Robert,  Claude, and Joseph Somerville

Alfred Hartley O'Connor, known as Alf, was the fifth child of James Daniel O'Connor and Alice Louisa Williams. He was born in Leeds on 28th January 1888.

He had one sister and five brothers. These were:-

Ethelina,  James,  Edward,  Robert,  Claude, and Joseph Somerville

The earliest reference to him is his Birth Certificate. When he was born the family were living at 16 Bywater Street, Leeds. His father worked as a Mill Labourer. His birth was registered by his mother who signed the Birth Certificate with an "X" instead of her name.

The next mention of him is in the 1891 Census. Alfred was 3 years old and they were living at 4 Bywater Street. His father worked as a Mechanical Engineer.

When the 1901 Census was taken he was 13 years old and working as a "Lakes Coalout". It isn't known what this occupation was, it may be an error in the transcription of the Census Return due to a misreading of the manuscript entry. He was living at 4 Bywater Street and his father was now working as a Joiner.
There had, however, been a change in the family, his mother had died the previous November.
