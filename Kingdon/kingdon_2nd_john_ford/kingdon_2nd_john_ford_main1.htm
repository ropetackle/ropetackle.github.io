Born: 1852 or 1853 at Meshaw, Devon
 
Parents: Albert Ford (mother unknown)
 
Wife: Susanna Kingdon
 
Stepson: James Kingdon
 
Children: Robert,  Sarah,  Ruth,  Esther,  Elizabeth,  Gertrude,  Eva,  Jessie,  Daisy,  Fredrick,  William

John Ford was born in Meshaw, Devon in 1852 or 1853. He was the son of Albert Ford, a Labourer. His mother is unknown.

John married Susanna Kingdon and became the Step-father of James Kingdon. John and Susanna had at least 11 children. These were:-

Robert, Sarah, Ruth, Esther, Elizabeth, Gertrude, Eva, Jessie, Daisy, Fredrick, and William

The earliest mention of John is in his Marriage Certificate when he married Susanna Kingdon on 17th April 1883. He was then 30 years old, living in South Street, South Molton and working as a Gardener.

The next mention of John is in the 1891 Census. He was now 38 years old, working as an Agricultural Labourer, and living with his family in  Morchard Bishop, Devon. The Census gives their address as Watcombe. This could have been a farm near the hamlet of Knightstone or Farthing Park which are about 2 kilometers east of Morchard Bishop. Living with him and Susanna were his stepson James, and their four children, Robert, Sarah, Ruth and Esther.

When the 1901 Census was taken John and his family were living at 1 Furzedown Cottages, Copplestone, in the Parish of Colebrook. He was 48 years old and still working as an Agricultural Labourer. Also living with him and Susanna were six of their children, Robert, Esther, Elizabeth, Gertrude, Eva and Jessie.

It is thought that John and Susanna had at least three more children, Daisy, Fredrick and William.
