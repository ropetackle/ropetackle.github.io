Relationship: James Kingdon is a Great-Great-Great-Great-Grandfather of Faye, Jenny and Kate Lloyd and of Chloe and Daniel Marshall.
 
Born: About 1822 or 1823 in Knowstone, Devon
 
Wife: Susanna Smith
 
Children: Susanna,  Jane

James Kingdon, an Agricultural Labourer, was born in Knowstone, Devon in about 1822 or 1823. Nothing is known about his parents.

James married Susanna Smith and they had at least 2 children, Susanna and Jane.

The earliest reference to James is in the Birth Certificate of his daughter Susanna who was born on 20th November 1862. He was working as a Farm Labourer and was living in East Street, South Molton, Devon.

When the 1881 Census was Taken James was living at 2 Bennetts Court, East Street, South Molton with his two daughters. He was 58 years old, a widower, his wife having died some time between 1870 and 1881, and was working as an Agricultural Labourer.

The next reference to James is in 1883 when Susanna married John Ford. Her Marriage Certificate describes him as a Labourer.

James was still living in South Molton when the 1891 Census was taken. He was living on his own, still at 2 Bennett Court in East Street. He was now 68 years old, and is still described as an Agricultural Labourer.

He may have died at South Molton in 1897 (Death Register to check - vol 5b, page269, Dec 1897)
