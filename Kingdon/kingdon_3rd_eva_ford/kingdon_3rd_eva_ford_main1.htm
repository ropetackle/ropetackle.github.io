Born: 1896 or 1897 in Colebrooke, Devon
 
Parents: John Ford and Susanna Kingdon
 
Stepbrother: James Kingdon
 
Brothers and Sisters: Robert,  Sarah,  Ruth,  Esther,  Elizabeth,  Gertrude,  Jessie,  Daisy,  Fredrick,  William

Eva Ford, was the seventh child of John Ford, an Agricultural Labourer, and Susanna Kingdon. According to the 1901 Census she was born in about 1896 or 1897 in Colebrooke, Devon. It is possible that she was actually born in Copplestone which is in the Parish of Colebrooke.

Eva had one stepbrother, James Kingdon, and at least 3 brothers and 7 sisters. These were:-

Robert, Sarah, Ruth, Esther, Elizabeth, Gertrude, Jessie, Daisy, Fredrick, and William

The only Reference to Eva is in the 1901 Census. She was 4 years old and living with her family at 1 Furzedown Cottages, Copplestone.
