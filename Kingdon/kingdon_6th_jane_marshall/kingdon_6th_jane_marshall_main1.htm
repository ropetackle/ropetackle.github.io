Born: 1960 at Chelmsford, Essex
 
Parents: Michael John Simmons and Helen Marjorie Kingdon
 
Brother: Ian David Marshall
 
Husband: Clive Andrew Lloyd
 
Children: Faye Nicola,  Jennifer Helen and Kate Elizabeth

Jane Imogen Simmons is the daughter of Helen Marjorie Kingdon and Michael John Simmons, a jazz pianist who performs under the name of Michael Garrick. She was born on 12th February 1960 at Chelmsford Hospital, Essex.

Jane's parents' marriage broke up and Jane and her mother went to live with Helen's parents who lived in Chadwell Heath, Essex at 14 Kings Avenue. 

In 1964 Jane's mother married Robin Marshall, and they went to live in Gloucestershire at Cinderford in the Forest Of Dean. Their bungalow was called "Maranatha" and was in Woodside Avenue.

Helen and Robin legally adopted Jane and she then became known as Jane Imogen Marshall.

In 1966 Jane left Gloucestershire when the family moved to Wiltshire. Her new home was in Cricklade at 27 Pauls Croft.

In 1971 Jane's family once again moved, this time to Ilkley, Yorkshire.

Jane married Clive Andrew Lloyd at Ilkley Parish Church on 9th May 1981.
