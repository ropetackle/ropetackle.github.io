Helen At 21

Relationship: Helen Marjorie Kingdon is a Grandmother of Faye, Jenny and Kate Lloyd and of Chloe and Daniel Marshall
 
Born: 1936 at Charing Cross, London
 
Parents: Edward John Kingdon and Marjorie Tate
 
Married: (1) Michael John Simmons
 (2) Robin Marshall
 
Children: Jane Imogen and Ian David

Helen Marjorie Kingdon is the only child of Edward John Kingdon, a Schoolteacher, and Marjorie Tate, who had also been a Schoolteacher. She was born on 3rd March 1936 in Charing Cross Hospital, London. As the hospital is within the sound of “Bow Bells” Helen is technically a Cockney.

When Helen was born her parents were living in Chadwell Heath, Essex at 129 Mill Lane.

Helen's first child, Jane Imogen, was born on 12th February 1960.

Helen married Jane’s father, Michael John Simmons at Chelmsford Register Office on the 12th March 1960. The marriage subsequently broke up and they were divorced, the divorce being made absolute on the 17th February 1964.

After her marriage broke up Helen lived with Jane and Helen's parents at their house at 14 Kings Avenue, Chadwell Heath, Essex. She went to work at May & Bakers, a Chemical Manufacturing Company at Dagenham, Essex as a Personnel Assistant and it was while she was working there that she met Robin Marshall who was employed there as a Works Chemist.

Helen and Robin married in 1964
