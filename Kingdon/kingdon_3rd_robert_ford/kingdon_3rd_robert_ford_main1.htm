Born: 1884 or 1885 in Romansleigh, Devon
 
Parents: John Ford and Susanna Kingdon
 
Stepbrother: James Kingdon
 
Brothers and Sisters:  Sarah,  Ruth,  Esther,  Elizabeth,  Gertrude,  Eva,  Jessie,  Daisy,  Fredrick,  William

Robert John Ford was the eldest child of John Ford, An Agricultural Labourer, and Susanna Kingdon. According to the 1891 Census he was born at Romansleigh, Devon in about 1884 or 1885.

Robert had one stepbrother, James Kingdon, and at least 2 brothers and 8 sisters. These were:-

Sarah, Ruth, Esther, Elizabeth, Gertrude, Eva, Jessie, Daisy, Fredrick, and William

The earliest reference to Robert is in the 1891 Census. He was 7 years old and living with his parents and family in  Morchard Bishop, Devon. The Census gives their address as Watcombe. This could have been a farm near the hamlet of Knightstone or Farthing Park which are about 2 kilometers east of Morchard Bishop.

The next mention of Robert is in the 1901 Census. He was now 17 years old, single and working as a Mason's Labourer.  He was still living with his family at 1 Furzedown Cottages, Copplestone in the Parish of Colebrooke, Devon.
