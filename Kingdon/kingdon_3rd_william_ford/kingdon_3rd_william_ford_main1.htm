Born: 1906 Copplestone, Devon
 
Parents: John Ford and Susanna Kingdon
 
Stepbrother: James Kingdon
 
Brothers and Sisters: Robert,  Sarah,  Ruth,  Esther,  Elizabeth,  Gertrude,  Eva,  Jessie,  Daisy,   Fredrick

William Ford is a son of John Ford, an Agricultural Labourer, and Susanna Kingdon. He was born in 1906  in Copplestone, in the Parish of Colebrooke, Devon.

He had one stepbrother, James Kingdon and at least 2 brothers and 8 sisters. These were:-

Robert, Sarah, Ruth, Esther, Elizabeth, Gertrude, Eva, Jessie, Daisy and Fredrick

All that is known about William is that he was born in 1906 and was the same age as James Kingdon’s son, Edward John  Kingdon. This meant that, although he was the same age as Edward John, he was his Uncle - something which Edward John Kingdon always thought a bit strange!
