Relationship: Susanna Smith is a Great-Great-Great-Great-Grandmother of Faye, Jenny and Kate Lloyd and of Chloe and Daniel Marshall.  
 
Husband: James Kingdon
 
Children: Susanna,  Jane
 
Died: Between 1870 and 1881

Susanna Smith is thought to have been born in the South Molton area of Devon. Nothing is known about when she was born. Neither is anything known about her parents.

Susanna married James Kingdon, a Farm Labourer, and they had at least 2 children, Susanna and Jane.

The only reference to Susanna is in the Birth Certificate of her daughter Susanna who was born on 20th November 1862. She was living in East Street, South Molton, Devon. When she registered the birth she signed the Register with an "X".

She appears to died some time between 1870 or 1871, when  her daughter Jane was born, and 1881 as the Census of that year describes her husband as a Widower. She may have died at South Molton in 1878 (Death Register to check - vol 5b, page 377, March 1878)
