Parents: John Ford and Susanna Kingdon
 
Stepbrother: James Kingdon
 
Brothers and Sisters:  Robert,  Sarah,  Ruth,  Esther,  Elizabeth,  Gertrude,  Eva,  Jessie,  Daisy,   William

Fredrick Ford is believed to be a son of John Ford, an Agricultural Labourer, and Susanna Kingdon. It is not known when or where he was born but it is most likely that he was born in Copplestone, in the Parish of Colebrooke, Devon after the 1901 Census as he is not mentioned in either the 1891 or 1901 Census Returns for his parents.

He had one stepbrother, James Kingdon and at least 2 brothers and 8 sisters. These were:-

Robert, Sarah, Ruth, Esther, Elizabeth, Gertrude, Eva, Jessie, Daisy, and William

The only evidence for his existence is that Edward John Kingdon believed his father James had a stepbrother called Fredrick.
