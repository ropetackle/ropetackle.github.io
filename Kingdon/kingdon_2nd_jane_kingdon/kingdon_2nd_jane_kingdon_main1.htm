Born: 1870 or 1871 in South Molton, Devon
 
Parents: James Kingdon and Susanna Smith
 
Sister: Susanna

Jane Kingdon was born in South Molton, Devon in about 1870 or 1871. Her parents were James Kingdon, a Farm Labourer, and Susanna Smith.

She had at least one sister, Susanna.

The only reference to Jane is in the 1881 Census. She was then 10 years old and living with her father and sister at 2 Bennetts Court, South Molton. Her mother had died.
