Born: 1885 or 1886 in Morchard Bishop, Devon
 
Parents: John Ford and Susanna Kingdon
 
Stepbrother: James Kingdon
 
Brothers and Sisters: Robert,  Ruth,  Esther,  Elizabeth,  Gertrude,  Eva,  Jessie,  Daisy,  Fredrick,  William

Sarah Ford was the second child of John Ford, An Agricultural Labourer, and Susanna Kingdon. According to the 1891 Census she was born at Morchard Bishop, Devon in about 1885 or 1886. It is possible that she was actually born at Watcombe which is in the Parish of Morchard Bishop.

Sarah had one stepbrother, James Kingdon, and at least 3 brothers and 7 sisters. These were:-

Robert, Ruth, Esther, Elizabeth, Gertrude, Eva, Jessie, Daisy, Fredrick, and William

The only reference to Sarah is in the 1891 Census. She was 5 years old and living with her parents and family in  Morchard Bishop, Devon.The Census gives their address as Watcombe. This could have been a farm near the hamlet of Knightstone or Farthing Park which are about 2 kilometers east of Morchard Bishop.

There is no mention of Sarah in the 1901 Census when she would have been 15. She may have left home and gone to work as a Servant somewhere or she may have died.
