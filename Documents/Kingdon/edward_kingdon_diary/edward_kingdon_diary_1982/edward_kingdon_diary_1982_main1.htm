April

Helen drove us to Stamford to stay with Jane and Andy in their new house 3, Fife Close, Stamford. Liked it and their furnishings. They have chosen well and they seem to have started where we left off – as it should be but rarely is in this world. Stayed a week. Helen and we enjoyed it immensely.

May

May 23rd

Joy and Albert have been with us since 16th. Returned today.

September

Sept 11-16th

Our second visit to Jane and Andy, again with Helen. Jane makes us so welcome. Enjoyed our visit. Did a bit of gardening. Planted some Leylandii with Jane’s help.

Sept 26th-Oct 2nd

Joy and Albert visited us. We had an enjoyable week. High light Burnsall – a beautiful day to visit such a lovely village. Photos taken, hope they come out well.

[This is the last entry in the diaries of Edward John Kingdon.]
