Letter 3 - 13th December 1910

Infantry Record Office,
Hamilton, N.B.
13th December 1910.

Madam,

As notified in my letter of 10th instant, you have been allocated passage to South Africa on Transport “Soudan” embarking at Southampton on 20th instant between the hours of 11 a.m. and 6 p.m.

Your Embarkation Returns, Army Form B.143 (two forms) are forwarded herewith.

A letter containing instructions as to Medical Examination is attached hereto.

On arrival at Southampton you are required to report to the Military Staff officer superintending the Embarkation, to whom you will hand the Embarkation Returns and Medical Certificate, Army Form B.155.

Please sign the attached certificate as to having received the above instructions and Army Forms B.143 & B.155, and return to me in enclosed stamped addressed envelope.

Yours faithfully,
L H Caird, Major
for Colonel i/c Records No. 2 District.

Miss F.Pepper,
12 Newthorpe St,
Nottingham.
