Title Description

Kingdon Family Papers

Diary of Edward John Kingdon: 1978 - 1982 The diary which he kept intermittently from October 1978 to September 1982
Diary of Edward John Kingdon: His Maternal Grandmother The entries from his diary which give his memories of his maternal grandmother, Mrs. Smith and her funeral
Diary of Edward John Kingdon: His Father The entries from his diary which give his memories of his father, James Kingdon
Diary of Edward John Kingdon: His Mother The entries from his diary which give his memories of his mother, Elizabeth Ann Smith Smith
Diary of Edward John Kingdon: Childhood The entries from his diary which describe his childhood in Devon
Diary of Edward John Kingdon: Jane's Wedding The entries from his diary which describe the wedding of his granddaughter, Jane Imogen Marshall, in May 1981
Diary of Edward John Kingdon: Literacy The entries from his diary which give his thoughts on literacy, particularly in the C19th.
Origin of The Kingdons Notes on the origin of the Kingdons in Devon and of their name

Smith Family Papers

The Smith Family: Letter From Olive Treloar - 1977 A letter from Olive Treloar  to Edward John Kingdon, her cousin, about their mothers' family, the Smith Family. A letter "written" in the Cornish accent.

Tate Family Papers

The Tate Family: Letter From Jack Coulthard - 1960 An extract from a letter from Jack Coulthard, cousin-in-law of Marjorie Tate, about the origins of the Tate Family
David Tate and Ashburnham Golf Club: 1898 to 1903 Extracts from the history of Ashburnham Golf Club with many references to David Tate's work there as Club Professional
Rickerby: Descriptions of the Village Various historical accounts of Rickerby, where David Tate was born, and the village's Census returns for 1881 and 1891
Robert Fredrick Tate's Death  

Marshall Family Papers

Lord Kitchener's Memo to British Troops in India: 1905 Thomas Marshall's copy of Lord Kitchener's memorandum to the British troops in India, warning of the danger of consorting with native women.
History of the 1st Battalion Royal Scots Fusiliers A history of the 1st Battalion Royal Scots Fusiliers.
Summary of Thomas Marshall's Army Career A table summarising his Army Postings, Promotions and Qualifications

Pepper Family Papers

Letters From The Army To Florence Pepper: 1910 Copies of the letters sent to Florence Pepper about her passage to South Africa to marry Thomas Marshall
