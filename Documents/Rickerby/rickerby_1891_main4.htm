1891 Census

When the 1891 Census was taken, the population of Rickerby was about 84 which can be compared with one of about 90 in 1881. From the description of the employments of many of the residents it can be seen how dependent on the MacInnes's of Rickerby House the local economy still was.
It is interesting to note that the largest family in the village, in terms of numbers, was still the Tate Family.
