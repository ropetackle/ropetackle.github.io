1881 Census

When the 1881 Census was taken, the population of Rickerby was about 90 which can be compared with one of about 80 in 1847. From the description of the employments of many of the residents it can be seen how dependent on the MacInnes's of Rickerby House the local economy was.
It is interesting to note that the largest family in the village, in terms of numbers, was the Tate Family.
