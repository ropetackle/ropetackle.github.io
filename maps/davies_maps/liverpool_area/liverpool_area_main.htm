This map of the Liverpool area shows the location of Hill Street where William Pennill and his wife Marion McAllister were living when their daughter Margaret Ann Pennill was born in 1853.

It also shows Elm Grove, where Edward Davies, his wife Margaret Ann Pennill and their son, my Grandpa Davies were living when the 1881 , 1891 and 1901 Census were taken. (Robin Marshall)
