When they left Princetown in about 1910 or 1911, James Kingdon, Elizabeth Ann Smith and their son Jack went to live at 4 Powderham Terrace, Newton Abbott (Grid Reference SX859708).

Jack live here until he went to teach in Dagenham. Elizabeth was living here when she died in 1929.
