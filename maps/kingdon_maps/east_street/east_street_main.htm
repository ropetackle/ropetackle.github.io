When Susanna Kingdon was born in 1862 her parents, James Kingdon and Susanna Smith, were living in East Street, South Molton, Devon.

When the 1881 Census was taken they were living at 2 Bennetts Court. This may have been in East Street as that was Susanna Kingdon's address when she married in 1883.
