The Kingdon family originated form the area of South Molton, North Devon (Grid Ref. SS715258). The first James Kingdon was born at Knowstone in about 1822 or 1823. His daughter Susanna was born in South Molton as was her son James.

Susanna's husband, John Ford was born in Meshaw and their eldest child, Robert, was born at Romansleigh.
