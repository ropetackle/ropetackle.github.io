Copplestone, North Devon (Grid Ref. SS770025) is where James Kingdon was brought up and where his mother and stepfather lived.

When the 1891 Census was taken James, his mother and his stepfather were living in the Parish of Morchard Bishop at Watcombe.

When the 1901 Census was taken James' mother and her husband were living in Copplestone at 1 Furzedown Cottages.
