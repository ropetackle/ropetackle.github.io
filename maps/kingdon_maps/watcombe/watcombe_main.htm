When the 1891 Census was taken, James Kingdon, his Mother Susanna Kingdon, her husband John Ford and four of their children were living at Watcombe, Morchard Bishop. It is thought that Watcome is a farm in the vicinity of Knightstone as Watcombe has the same Post Code as Knightstone House and Farthing Park.
