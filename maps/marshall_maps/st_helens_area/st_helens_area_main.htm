This map shows various places in the St Helens area.

Grandpa and Grandma Davies were married at Eccleston Parish Church and first lived in Edge Street. They moved to Palatine Villas where my Mother was born and then lived at Fireman's Cottage. When Grandpa retired they first lived in Speakman Road and then at Berringtons Lane, Rainford.

My Mother and Father were married at Rainhill Parish Church. When they returned to St Helens from Glasgow they lived in The Grove. From there they moved to Dentons Green Lane and then to Higher Lane, Rainford. (Robin Marshall)
