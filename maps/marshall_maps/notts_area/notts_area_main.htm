This map shows the location of the various places in and around Nottingham which are associated with the Marshall or Pepper Families.

Basford is where Morrisson Hodges and his wife Ann Crump lived and where their daughter Ann was born.

High Pavement is where Mary Lamb was living when she married William Marshall.

Meadows is where Florence Pepper was living when she became engaged to Thomas Marshall.

Wilford is where Thomas Marshall lived before he joined the Army
