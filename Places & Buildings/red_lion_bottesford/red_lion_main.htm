This photograph shows the Red Lion Public House which is on the corner of Church Street and Grantham Road, Easthorpe, Bottesford, Leicestershire (Grid Reference SK 807390). The picture is taken looking up Church Street.

When the 1841 Census was taken, Edward Lamb, who was a Publican, his wife Ann Lamb and daughter Mary Lamb were living in Church Street, Easthorpe, Bottesford.
Twenty years later, when the 1861 Census was taken, Mary Lamb, her husband William Marshall, their five children and Mary's mother, Ann Lamb, were living The Beershop, Easthorpe, Bottesford. It is possibly that they were living at the premises where her father had been the Publican before he died.

Although no further information is given as to either of the addresses, it might be that they were living on the site of what is now this Public House. (Robin Marshall)
