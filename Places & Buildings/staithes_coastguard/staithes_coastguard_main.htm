This photo shows the Coastguard Cottages in the village of Staithes, North Yorkshire. When the 1881 Census was taken William Roberts, his wife Fanny Cooper, their daughter Frances Roberts and two sons, William and James were living in Staithes, possibly in one of these houses. William Roberts was the Command Boatman of the Coastguard at Staithes. (Robin Marshall)
