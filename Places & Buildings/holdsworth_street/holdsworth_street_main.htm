25 Holdsworth Street, Pennycomequick, Plymouth (Grid Reference SX 474554) is where Edward John Kingdon was born in 1906. He and his parents, James Kingdon and Elizabeth Ann Smith, lived here until they moved to Princetown on Dartmoor.

It is very close to the main Plymouth Railway Station and was handy for his father who would have had to get to work very early to prepare his steam engine for the day's work (Robin Marshall)
