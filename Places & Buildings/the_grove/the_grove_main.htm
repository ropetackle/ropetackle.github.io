4 The Grove, Bleak Hill, St Helens is where we lived from 1939 to 1946 and where Alan and Brian were born.
 
One winter during the War we had so much snow that it was level with the top of the front garden wall. I can remember the milkman delivering the milk with a horse-drawn "sleigh" - I think he had taken the wheels off the cart he normally used.
(Robin Marshall)
