158 Speakman Road, St Helens, (Grid Reference SJ499964) is where my Grandpa and Grandma Davies lived when he retired and they left their cottage at the Rainhill Hospital.

My Uncle Bill also lived here and after he had married and started a family Grandma and Grandpa moved to the isolated cottage in Berringtons Lane, Rainford where Grandpa lived until he died.

Grandpa Davies always loved his garden and the one he had in the hospital grounds was very big. He grew the most wonderful vegetables and soft fruit. Living here he really missed his garden and this was one reason why they moved to a cottage in the country. (Robin Marshall)
