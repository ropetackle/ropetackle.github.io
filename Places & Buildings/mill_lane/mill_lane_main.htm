Mum, standing by the gate waving, and Dad, leaning on the fence, as newly-weds outside their first house, 129 Mill Lane, Chadwell Heath (Grid Reference TQ 483884).
This is the house I came home to after my birth in Charing X Hospital.

During the war tank-traps were erected outside on the pavement. Very good for jumping on and for playing in the sand stored between them, but not much of a defence against German armies I fear. (Helen Kingdon)
