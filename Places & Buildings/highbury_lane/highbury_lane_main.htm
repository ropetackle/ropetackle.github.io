5 Highbury Lane, Headingley, Leeds (Grid Reference SE 283368) is where David Tate and Francis Martha Cleo Roberts lived after they were married in 1894 and where their first child, Florence, was born in 1895.

It is a typical "back to back" terrace house. I suspect that the lavatories were outside, in the space on the right of the photograph, between their terrace and the next, and that they were shared by the houses in two terraces on either side of them. (Robin Marshall)
