St Ann, The Parish Church Of Rainhill, (Grid Reference SJ493912), is where my Mother and Father were married on the 19th June 1937.

Mum particularly wanted to be married at this Church but unfortunately her parents' house was not in the Rainhill Parish. She therefore left a suitcase at the home of a friend who lived in the Parish and gave that address as hers. It is this address which is shown on her Marriage Certificate. (Robin Marshall)
