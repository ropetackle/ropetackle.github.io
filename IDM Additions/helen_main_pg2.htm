Later Years

1964 Onwards

 

A photo of Mum relevant to period

When she married Robin Marshall in 1964 they could not, because of the high cost of housing, afford to buy a house in the London Area. Robin therefore changed jobs, moving from May & Bakers to Engelhards, an American firm who manufactured Precious Metal chemical compounds, at Cinderford in the Forest of Dean, Gloucestershire. There they bought a small bungalow, “Maranatha”, Woodside Avenue, Cinderford.

On the26th August 1965 her second child, a son, Ian David was born.
