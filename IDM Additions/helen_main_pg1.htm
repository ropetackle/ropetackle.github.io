Born: 3rd March 1936 in Charing Cross Hospital, London.

Parents: Edward Kingdon and Marjorie Tate.

Husband: Robin Marshall

Children: Jane and Ian

Helen Marjorie Kingdon in 1957 aged 21

Helen is the only child of Edward John Kingdon, a Schoolteacher, and Marjorie Tate.

She was born on 3rd March 1936 in Charing Cross Hospital, London. The hospital is within the sound of “Bow Bells” and she is therefore a Cockney.

Her father was an Elementary Schoolteacher and her mother had also been a teacher. When she was born they were living at 129 Mill Lane, Chadwell Heath, Essex.

Her first child was her daughter Jane Imogen Simmons (Marshall) who was born on 12th February 1960.

Helen married Jane’s father, Michael John Simmons at Chelmsford Register Office on the 12th March 1960. The marriage subsequently broke up and they were divorced, the divorce being made absolute on the 17th February 1964.

After the marriage broke up Helen lived with her parents and Jane at her parents house at 14 Kings Avenue, Chadwell Heath, Essex and she started working at May & Bakers, a Chemical Manufacturing Company at Dagenham, Essex as a Personnel Assistant. It was here that she met Robin Marshall who was employed there as a Works Chemist.
