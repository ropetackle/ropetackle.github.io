Born: Between 1815 and 1816 in Cornwall.
 
Parents: Thomas Pike and Elizabeth (maiden name unknown).
 
Sisters and Brother: Elizabeth,  Ann,  Thomas

Diana Pike was born in Cornwall, probably in the Penryn Area, between 1815 and 1816. She was a daughter of Thomas Pike, a Tallow Chandler, and his wife Elizabeth.

Diana is thought to have had at least two sisters and one brother. These were:-

Elizabeth, Ann, and Thomas.

The only reference to Diana is in the 1841 Census.  She was 25 years old and living with her mother and father in the High Street, Penryn. 

The reasons for thinking that she was the sister of Elizabeth Pike are:-

(1) Her mother was called Elizabeth
(2) Her father's family were the only Pikes in Penryn in 1841
(3) The younger Elizabeth Pike was 40 when the 1841 Census was taken and this would tie in with the age of Diana's sister Ann who was 30 when the Census was taken.
(3) The husband of the younger Elizabeth, John Smith was a Tailor as was Diana's brother Thomas.
