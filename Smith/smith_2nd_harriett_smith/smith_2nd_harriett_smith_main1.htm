Born: 1835 or 1836 in Cornwall
 
Parents: John Smith and Elizabeth Pike
 
Brothers: Edward,  John

Harriett Smith was born in Cornwall, most likely in Helston, in 1835 or 1836. She was the daughter of John Smith, a Tailor, and Elizabeth Pike.

Harriett had at least two brothers. These were:

Edward Thomas and John

The only mention of Harriett is in the 1841 Census. She was 5 years old and living with her parents and brother Edward in Wendron Street, Helston
