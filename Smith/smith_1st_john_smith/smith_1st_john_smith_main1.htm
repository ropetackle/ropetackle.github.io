Relationship: John Smith is a Great-Great-Great-Great-Grandfather of Faye, Jenny and Kate Lloyd and of Chloe and Daniel Marshall.
 
Born: Cornwall between 1800 and 1801
 
Wife: Elizabeth Pike
 
Children: Harriett,   Edward Thomas,   John

John Smith was born in Cornwall in about 1800 or 1801, possibly in the Falmouth area. Nothing is known about his parents.

John married Elizabeth Pike and they had at least three children. These were:

Harriett, Edward Thomas and John.

The earliest reference to John is in the Birth Certificate of his son, Edward Thomas who was born on 11th March 1838. John was working as a Tailor and living in Wendron Street, Helston, Cornwall.

When the 1841 Census was taken John was still living at Wendron Street and working as a Tailor. Living with him was his wife, his daughter Harriet and son Edward.

John seems to have died some time between about 1845 and 1861 as when the 1861 Census was taken Elizabeth was a widow living at Wendron Street with her 15 year old son John.
