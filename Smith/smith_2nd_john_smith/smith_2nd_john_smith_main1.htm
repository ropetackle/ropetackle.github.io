Born: 1845 or 1846 in Helston, Cornwall
 
Parents: John Smith and Elizabeth Pike
 
Brothers and Sisters: Harriett,  Edward

John Smith was born in Helston, Cornwall in 1845 or 1846. He was a son of John Smith, a Tailor, and Elizabeth Pike.

John had at least one sister and one brother. These were:

Harriett and Edward Thomas

The only mention of John is in the 1861 Census. He was 15 years old, employed as an Apprentice Shoemaker and living with his widowed Mother in Wendron Street, Helston
