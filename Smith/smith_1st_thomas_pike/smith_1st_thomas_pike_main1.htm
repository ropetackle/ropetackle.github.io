Born: Between 1820 and 1821 in Cornwall.
 
Parents: Thomas Pike and Elizabeth (maiden name unknown).
 
Sisters : Elizabeth,  Ann,  Diana

Thomas Pike was born in Cornwall, probably in the Penryn Area, between 1815 and 1816. He was the son of Thomas Pike, a Tallow Chandler, and his wife Elizabeth.

Thomas is thought to have had at least three sisters. These were:-

Elizabeth, Ann, and Diana.

The only reference to Thomas is in the 1841 Census.  He was 20 years old working as a Tailor and living with his mother and father in the High Street, Penryn. 

The reasons for thinking that he was the brother of Elizabeth Pike are:-

(1) His mother was called Elizabeth
(2) His father's family were the only Pikes in Penryn in 1841
(3) The younger Elizabeth Pike was 40 when the 1841 Census was taken and this would tie in with the age of Thomas's sister Ann who was 30 when the Census was taken.
(3) The husband of the younger Elizabeth, John Smith was a Tailor as was Thomas.
