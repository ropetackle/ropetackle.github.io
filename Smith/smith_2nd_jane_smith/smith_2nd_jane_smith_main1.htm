Born: Cornwall in about 1834 or 1835
 
Parents: James Smith, and Matilda Smith (maiden name unknown)
 
Sister: Elizabeth

Jane Smith was born in Cornwall, probably in Cadgwith, in about 1834 or 1835. She was a daughter of James Smith, a Fisherman, and his wife Matilda.

Jane had at least one sister, Elizabeth.

The only reference to Jane is in the 1841 Census. She was 7 years old and living with her parents and younger sister Elizabeth in Cadgwith, Ruan Minor, Cornwall. Also living with them was a 75 years old lodger, Henry Trewise. Henry might have been a relation of her parents, possibly even Mother's father.
