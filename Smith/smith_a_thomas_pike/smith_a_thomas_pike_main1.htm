Relationship: Thomas Pike is believed to have been a Great-Great-Great-Great-Great-Grandfather of Faye, Jenny and Kate Lloyd and of Chloe and Daniel Marshall.
 
Born: Between 1775 and 1776 in Cornwall.
 
Wife: Elizabeth (Maiden name not known)
 
Children: Elizabeth,  Ann,  Diana,  Thomas

Thomas Pike is thought to have been born in Cornwall in the Penryn Area between 1775 and 1776. Nothing is known about his parents.

Thomas married Elizabeth (maiden name unknown). It is thought that Thomas was the Father of Elizabeth Pike who was born in Penryn between 1799 and 1801. Thomas and Elizabeth had at least three other children. These were:-

Ann, Diana, and Thomas.

The only reference to Thomas is in the 1841 Census.  He was 65 years old and living with his wife and three of their children, Ann, Diana and Thomas, in the High Street, Penryn. The Census Return gives his occupation as that of a Tallow Chandler.

The reasons for thinking that hr was the Father of Elizabeth Pike are the fact that:-

(1) His wife was called Elizabeth
(2) Thomas and Elizabeth's family were the only Pikes in Penryn in 1841
(3) The younger Elizabeth Pike was 40 when the 1841 Census was taken and this would tie in with the ages of Thomas's children as given in the 1841 Census, Ann was 30, Diana 25 and Thomas 20
(3) The husband of the younger Elizabeth, John Smith was a Tailor as was the younger Thomas Pike.
