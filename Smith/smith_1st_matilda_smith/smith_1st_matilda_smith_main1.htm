Relationship: Matilda Smith is a Great-Great-Great-Great-Grandmother of Faye, Jenny and Kate Lloyd and of Chloe and Daniel Marshall.
 
Born: Cornwall in about 1805 or 1806
 
Husband: James Smith
 
Children: Jane,   Elizabeth

Matilda Smith (maiden name unknown) was born in Cornwall in about 1805 or 1806,  probably in the area around Cadgwith or Ruan Minor on the Lizard Peninsula. Nothing is known about her parents.

Matilda married James Smith, a Fisherman, and they had at least two children. These were:-

Jane and Elizabeth.

The only reference to Matilda is in the 1841 Census. She was 35 years old and living with her husband and two children in Cadgwith, Ruan Minor, Cornwall. Also living with them was a 75 years old lodger, Henry Trewise. Henry might have been a relation of James or Matilda, possibly even her father.
