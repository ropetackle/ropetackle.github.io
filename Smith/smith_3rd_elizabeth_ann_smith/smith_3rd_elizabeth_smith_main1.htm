Introduction and Links To Her Other Pages

Elizabeth Ann Smith is a Great-Great-Grandmother of Faye, Jenny and Kate Lloyd and of Chloe and Daniel Marshall.

 

Elizabeth Ann Smith In About 1890

Introduction

Elizabeth Ann Smith was the daughter of Edward Thomas Smith, a Master Tailor, and his wife Elizabeth. She was born in Falmouth between 1870 and 1875.

She had two brothers and two sisters. These were:-

  • Edward Thomas John
  • Emma Jane
  • Harriet Katey
  • Richard

She married James Kingdon on 5th September 1901 and they had one child, Edward John.

She died on 8th July 1929.
